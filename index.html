The Reality of Plastics

5g

Weekly Ingestion

Humans ingest roughly 5g of microplastics weekly—the mass of a credit card. They have now been detected in human blood and placenta.

0%

The Compostability Myth

Promised biodegradation often fails to occur in stipulated timeframes. Instead, they disintegrate, leaving micro and nanoplastics in both marine and soil environments.

200M

Carbon Opportunity

200 million metric tons of untapped carbon exist as waste. We can rewire microbial pathways to upcycle this hazard into industrial feedstock.
